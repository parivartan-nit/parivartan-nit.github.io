Welcome to Parivartan

PARIVARTAN is an upcoming ISKCON Youth Project at NIT, Calicut, Kerala. The Project is intended to impart Krsna Consciousness to the bright young students of NIT in a modern and applicative fashion. It is inspired by the teachings of His Divine Grace A C Bhakti Vedanta Swami Prabhupada.

The project is steered under the guidance of HH Jayapataka Swami Maharaja and conducted under the supervision of HH Bhakti Vinoda Swami Maharaja. Few alumni of NIT Calicut have come forward and generously contributed towards this noble cause. With that the foundation for the institution has been completed and structural work for ground floor is in progress. Some of the recent photographs have been shown here.

Appreciation Letter from Guru Maharaj

Click to read

It gives me immense pleasure to introduce to our ISKCON Youth project – “PARIVARTAN” at the prestigious NIT Calicut, Kerala. “PARIVARTAN” is aimed at presenting brilliant illuminations of Krishna Consciousness philosophy to the youth in modern and applicative way. Today’s youth is intelligent and yet confused about the purpose and meaning of life.

What better panacea can be offered other than a centre like this? Srila Prabhupada and our Guru Maharaja have always been very encouraging for youth projects. I earnestly request our devotees and well-wishers to be big hearted enough to help our great cause. “PARIVARTAN” will always remain a bright jewel in the history of ISKCON.
