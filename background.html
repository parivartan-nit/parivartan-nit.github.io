Hare Kṛṣṇa

Please accept our humble obeisance. All glories to Srila Prabhupada. All glories to Sri Guru and Sri Gauranga.

“Parivartan” – the ISKCON youth project at NIT- Calicut has an interesting background. Back in late 90s and early 2000, HG Sarvaisvarya Dasa (now HH Bhakti Vinoda Swami) would regularly come to NIT (then REC, Calicut) and had introduced Krishna Consciousness to the students of college. Many students became dedicated followers of ISKCON and even got initiated.

The students inspired thus, encouraged their juniors as well and hence a band of students became quite popularly known as “Hare Kṛṣṇas” were created at the college. Under constant guidance from HG Sarvaisvarya Dasa (now HH Bhakti Vinoda Swami) the preaching at the college flourished.

Bhoomi Pooja

History

It was in 2005, that students realized that there should be a center of our own. A center where the interested students could come, chant, dance, read Srila Prabhupada’s books and take sumptuous prasadam and become serious in Krsna Consciousness. Hence the idea of “PARIVARTAN” – an academy for Personal change and inner transformation was conceived. A small piece of land – just adjacent to the campus was bought under the inspiration of HH Jayapataka Swami Maharaja and HH Bhakti Vinoda Swami. Since then, we alumni devotees of NIT, have dedicatedly and single handedly pursued the project and have brought the present status of construction.

Please visit other pages to know more details. We sincerely hope that devotees worldwide will understand the impact and contribution of such a center in the lives of young students. Please help us to achieve this small but insignificant contribution to Srila Prabhupada and ISKCON.
