Parivartan: The total Area is 1500sq ft, or 7.96 cents and has Three Floors.

Ground Floor — Radha Rani’s Kitchen + Prasadam Dining area (for 100 students)

Well furnished Kitchen and a dining hall to offer tasty Prasadam to the students will be in the ground floor. The capacity is approximately 100 students at a time. Prasadam is non-different from Krsna and Srila Prabhupada called Krsna Consciousness as "Kitchen Religion". He started ISKCON simply by giving tasty prasadam to the students.

First Floor — Lecture Hall + Library

The first floor will have a spacious lecture hall to accommodate 100+ students to hold classes, seminars and workshops on Krsna Consciousness Training. It will be equipped with modern amenities to present the philosophy in an appealing fashion.

The floor will also house a library where entire collection of Srila Prabhupada’s books, lectures, videos and other KC philosophy videos which the students can avail and benefit. Srila Prabhupada stressed understanding the Philosophy thorough science and logic and this set up is aimed at realizing it.

Second Floor — Guest Room + Student Dormitory

The second floor will have a student dormitory where serious students practicing Krsna Consciousness can stay and continue their academic as well as spiritual life.

In this way like minded students can stay together, attend mangala arati, take prasadam and side by side pursue their studies as well. Since "Parivartan" center is very close to campus, it is easy to commute. There will also be guest rooms where visiting devotees or parents or donors can stay.
